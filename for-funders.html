Honest about where we are.

We are forming as a CIC. We are not taking money yet. But if you fund this kind of work, we want to start a conversation now.

// 02 — what we are building toward

Technology that serves people, not the other way around.

We are a not-for-profit forming as a community interest company. Any surplus goes back into the work. We are not building toward an exit. We are building toward a sustainable organisation that keeps making things worth making.

Our first project is a community events platform for Birmingham. Accessible from day one. Open source throughout.
