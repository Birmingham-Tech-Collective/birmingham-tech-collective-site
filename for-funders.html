// 01 — for funders

Funding that fits what we are.

We are not looking for investment. We are looking for funders who want to support a small not-for-profit doing careful, open work in Birmingham.

  • // values.md

    What we will and will not do

    We will not take funding that puts commercial interests above community ones. We will not promise outcomes we cannot deliver. We will not rush. We publish our finances, our code, and our decisions in the open.

  • // structure.md

    Our structure

    We are forming as a Community Interest Company. A CIC cannot pay dividends to shareholders — any surplus goes back into the mission. We are registering in England and Wales.

  • // looking-for.md

    What we are looking for

    Grants from foundations, trusts, and public bodies that fund tech-for-good, digital inclusion, or community infrastructure. We are not looking for sponsorship or brand partnerships.

// 02 — where we are

Where we are.

We are in early conversations with funders. We are not yet taking money. If you are a funder or a foundation that supports this kind of work, we would like to hear from you now so we can have a proper conversation before we are ready to apply.
