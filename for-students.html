// 01 — for students

A real route into the industry.

Real open-source experience, mentorship, and credit for the work. Not a placement where you make spreadsheets.

// 02 — what you get

  • // experience.md

    Work on a live project

    Contribute to a real piece of open-source software being used by community organisations in Birmingham. Your commits are real. Your name is in the history.

  • // mentorship.md

    Mentorship from practitioners

    We pair contributors with experienced developers for code review, feedback, and honest guidance. Not a one-off chat — ongoing support through the project.

  • // credit.md

    Final-year project briefs

    We partner with Birmingham City University, Aston, and the University of Birmingham. If you are a course leader looking for industry briefs for your cohort, we want to talk.

// 03 — who we work with

Birmingham universities and colleges.

We are in conversation with Birmingham City University, Aston University, and the University of Birmingham about final-year project briefs and student placements. If your institution is not on this list and you think there is a fit, get in touch.
