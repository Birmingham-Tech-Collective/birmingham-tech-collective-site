// 01 — the platform

Our first build: a community events platform.

Organisers can post events with clear accessibility information. Attendees can find what is actually near them. Everything is keyboard- and screen-reader-friendly from day one, not as an afterthought.

// 02 — the problem

Community groups make do with tools that were not built for them.

Event listings are scattered across Facebook groups, Eventbrite pages, and local noticeboards. Accessibility information is missing or inconsistent. Finding out whether a venue has step-free access or a hearing loop should not require three phone calls.

We are building one place where community organisers in Birmingham can list events properly, and where anyone can find what is accessible to them.
